What makes a great website?

A website thats works for a business has traffic driven to it through carefully researched keywords and copywriting, keeps visitors through engaging content and has a great user experience which minimises click through rate.

Responsive

Your site looks good on all devices from large monitors to mobile - especially mobile! Over 50% of web pages are acessed via mobile.

Content management

My sites are managed using wordpress. You the client can easily add content and update your website. A website that has regular new content added will bring visitors back to the site and increase the ranking in search engines.

Optimised

Your site will be search engine optimsed and built to have fastest possible load times through testing to ensure compiance across all browsers
